FAQ

Typical Web Design & Digital Marketing Questions

How does your Digital Marketing Process works?

Research. Strategy. Action. Tracking Growth. All of our digital marketing and website development campaigns follow these steps. Research is the first step in properly assessing our clients' pain points and growth plans in relation to their current competitive industry landscape. Next, you must devise a strategy that overcomes any potential obstacles along the way to achieving the revenue goals of your client. Implementing that strategy quickly, strategically, and efficiently is the third step. Accountability is the last step — tracking our performance against key performance indicators and adjusting our plan of action as needed.

What industries do you specialize in?

Our online marketing agency has more than a decade of experience serving clients in a vast range of industries as a full-service web development agency. In addition to working with B2B and B2C eCommerce, we provide services to manufacturers, medical providers, real estate agents, senior living facilities, legal professionals, and many other businesses. We strive to deliver real, tangible results, so we will never take on a project, or client within an industry sector until we can demonstrate how we will achieve that.

Do you offer a Guarantee for you Digital Marketing services?

Choosing the right web development and internet marketing agency is no doubt one of the most important decisions you'll make. Ideally, an agency will work under your direction, be transparent, and stand by the work it does. That's what sets us apart. Our online marketing company is among the only ones to guarantee the quality of our work. Yep. We stand behind our team's work, guaranteeing results for select clients. When we provide our guaranteed package we start the work at 50% of the contract budget and when we achieve the planned results for two consecutive months, we move to the full rate we had negotiated. Find out if you qualify by contacting us.

What's the advantage of hiring Our Digital Agency instead of one of your many competitors?

It may seem biased, but we have a world-class team, each expert in their own field -- from SEO to web design, content to business strategy, and more. As a client, you will also have the benefit of not outsourcing your web development or marketing. Our team does all our work in-house, so we can provide more cost-effective, and most importantly, convergent growth strategies for your revenue. We stand by the work of our team. For select clients, we offer a guarantee package. We begin work with 50% of the budget until we achieve targets for two consecutive months, at which point we switch to full, agreed-upon rates. Find out if you qualify by contacting us.

How much do your services cost?

Each business is unique, and every marketing campaign has many factors to consider. Would you prefer a gradual but steady approach to growing your business, or an aggressive one? Is the main function of the website to provide information or will it include many complex features? This will all affect your overall costs. A high-performance website can cost anywhere from $10,000 to $40,000. Digital marketing solutions vary in price depending on where you want to take your business and how well you build your website (this is why it is so important to put effort into building your website). The monthly costs vary from $2,500 to over $10,000 for some clients. On average, we spend $4,000 a month on marketing.

How long will it take to start seeing improvements in sales?

Typically, you will see results in a relatively short amount of time after your web design and development project is completed. However, certain aspects of your digital marketing campaign are long-term games. We typically see tangible results from SEO within 90 days, followed by a steady increase in your sales from there on out. PPC campaigns typically yield quicker results and you can start seeing them in only a month. But keep in mind that every modality has its place in your overall marketing strategy, so never disregard SEO or content because of the longer ramp-up period. We would be happy to explain the reasons for this in person or over the phone during your consultation.
